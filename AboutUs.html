About Kpop

Kpop or K-pop is short for Korean Pop music. Despite the use of pop, Kpop is not restricted to just pop music. Kpop is a broad term for the Korean music industry. Kpop has many different genres of music. Some popular genres of Kpop are pop, rock ,and oldies music. Groups that fit these genres are:

  • BTS
  • Dreamcatcher
  • SNSD

Kpop started off only in Korea but as of late Kpop has been noticed more internationally. Many companies take notice of this and now specially create kpop groups to get a more international clientele.
