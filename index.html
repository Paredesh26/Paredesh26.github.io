Rosé Speaks On Past Relationships As a Kpop Idol

With the release of her latest album, Rosé reveals some information regarding her past relationships she had during her time in the Kpop group, Blackpink. She goes on to say that for the hardships she faced while being in some relationships that really tore her down, she looked amazing on the outside.

In the Kpop industry, idols dating is a bit taboo. Many fans can get really upset thus leading companies to make their idols break up to stop the controversy over them dating. That is why many idols hide their relationships. Relating to Rosé, she said the statement above to show how she had to hide her relationships thus not being able to openly express her pain of her heartbreaks.
